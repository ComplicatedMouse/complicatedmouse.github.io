◆ MirroringMode

enum nn::g3d::ResSkeleton::MirroringMode

Flag indicating the calculation method for motion mirroring.

Enumerator
MirroringMode_X 

The direction of the mirror is along the x-axis.

MirroringMode_XY 

The mirror direction is along the x-axis in the first half, and along the y-axis in the second half.

MirroringMode_XZ 

The mirror direction is along the x-axis in the first half, and along the z-axis in the second half.
