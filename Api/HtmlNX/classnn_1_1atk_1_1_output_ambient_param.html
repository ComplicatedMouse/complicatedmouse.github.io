◆ SetSurroundPan()

void nn::atk::OutputAmbientParam::SetSurroundPan ( float  surroundPan)

Sets the amount of relative change in the surround pan (front and rear position) for the particular output line.

Details
Pitch does not change when 0.0 is specified. If 1.0 is specified, the sound positioned at the front becomes the sound positioned in the center. If 2.0 is specified, the sound positioned at the front becomes the sound positioned at the back. Specify a negative number if you want to move the location toward the front.

Parameters
[in]surroundPanAmount of relative change in the surround pan (front and back position) for the particular output line.
