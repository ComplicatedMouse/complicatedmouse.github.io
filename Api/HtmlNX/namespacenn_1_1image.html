◆ JpegSamplingRatio

enum nn::image::JpegSamplingRatio

JPEG data sampling ratios.

Details
Represents the sampling ratios that can be specified for JPEG encoding. In general, picture quality increases and more data is output in order of 420, 422, and 444.

Enumerator
JpegSamplingRatio_444 

Sampling uses a YCbC ratio of 4:4:4.

JpegSamplingRatio_422 

Sampling uses a YCbC ratio of 4:2:2.

JpegSamplingRatio_420 

Sampling uses a YCbC ratio of 4:2:0.

◆ ExifOrientation

enum nn::image::ExifOrientation

Image orientation.

Details
Represents the image orientation, which is one piece of JPEG metadata.

Note that the item labeled "Rotate" indicates whether the image is rotated, and not whether the capture device was rotated when the image was captured. Signs are reversed when you consider the rotation angle of the capture device. Also, values represent rotation in the clockwise direction.

Enumerator
ExifOrientation_Normal 

Not rotated.

ExifOrientation_FlipHorizontal 

Flipped horizontally.

ExifOrientation_Rotate180 

Rotated 180 degrees.

ExifOrientation_FlipVertical 

Flipped vertically.

ExifOrientation_FlipTopRightToLeftBottom 

Flipped along the axis from upper left to lower right.

ExifOrientation_Rotate270 

Rotated clockwise 270 degrees.

ExifOrientation_FlipTopLeftToRightBottom 

Flipped along the axis from upper right to lower left.

ExifOrientation_Rotate90 

Rotated clockwise 90 degrees.

Examples
ImageJpegDecoding/ImageJpegDecoding.cpp, and ImageJpegEncoding/ImageJpegEncoding.cpp.
