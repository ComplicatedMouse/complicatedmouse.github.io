uint8_t nn::ui2d::ResPane::basePosition

The base position.

(The H and V of the base position, and the H and V for the origin of the parent pane. Each is 2 bits.) The H type is nn::ui2d::HorizontalPosition, and the Y type is nn::ui2d::VerticalPosition.
