bool nn::g3d::viewer::ShaderAssignUpdatedArg::MaterialShaderInfo::isOptimizationSkipped

Determines whether the creation of an optimized shader for the target material was skipped.

When Follow the material changes and recreate the shader is disabled for the target material in 3DEditor, the step that re-creates shaders for this material is skipped when shaders are re-created in 3DEditor due to changes in other materials. As a result, this flag will be true.
