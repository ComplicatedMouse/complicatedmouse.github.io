void nn::g3d::ShapeAnimBlender::ApplyTo ( ModelObj *  pModelObj) const

Applies the blend result to the target.

Parameters
[in]pModelObjPointer to the model to apply the blend results to.

Details
It does not apply to shapes with a cumulative weight of 0. Shapes that do not have a cumulative weight of 1 are normalized before the blending result is applied. The blend results themselves are not normalized.
