enum nn::g3d::ResBone::Flag

The flag for bones.

Enumerator
Flag_Visibility 

Default value for visibility.

Flag_BillboardNone 

Does not perform billboard calculations.

Flag_BillboardChild 

Performs computation as the child of a hierarchical billboard.

Flag_BillboardWorldViewVector 

Performs billboard calculations with World rules.

Flag_BillboardWorldViewPoint 

Performs billboard calculations with WorldViewpoint rules.

Flag_BillboardScreenViewVector 

Performs billboard calculations with Screen rules.

Flag_BillboardScreenViewPoint 

Performs billboard calculations with ScreenViewpoint rules.

Flag_BillboardYaxisViewVector 

Performs billboard calculations with YAxis rules.

Flag_BillboardYaxisViewPoint 

Performs billboard calculations with YAxisViewpoint rules.

Flag_SegmentScaleCompensate 

The use of internal features is prohibited.

Calculates with MayaSSC.

Flag_ScaleUniform 

The Scale is uniform.

Flag_ScaleVolumeOne 

The Scale volume is 1.

Flag_RotateZero 

Rotate is 0.

Flag_TranslateZero 

Translate is 0.

Flag_ScaleOne 

The Scale is 1.

Flag_RotTransZero 

Rotate and Translate are 0.

Flag_Identity 

Transformation is the unit matrix.

Flag_HiScaleUniform 

The cumulative scale is isotropic.

Flag_HiScaleVolumeOne 

The accumulated Scale volume is 1.

Flag_HiRotateZero 

The accumulated Rotate is 0.

Flag_HiTranslateZero 

The accumulated Translate is 0.

Flag_HiScaleOne 

The accumulated Scale is 1.

Flag_HiRotTransZero 

The accumulated Rotate and Translate are 0.

Flag_HiIdentity 

The accumulated transform is the identity matrix.

Flag_BillboardIndexNone 

No bones are affected by the hierarchical billboard.

enum nn::g3d::ResBone::MirroringState

The use of internal features is prohibited. The flag that indicates the bone position information. Used to calculate motion mirroring.

Enumerator
MirroringState_CenterRotate 

The center bone before rotation.

MirroringState_CenterPostRotate 

The center bone with the entered rotation value.

MirroringState_SideRoot 

The center bone after rotation and the changed orientation of local coordinates.

MirroringState_Side 

The root of the left/right bones created by mirroring the joint.
