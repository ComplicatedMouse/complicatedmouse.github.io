Changing Pitch

1. Overview

This page describes how to configure pitch in SoundMaker and AtkPlayer.

2. Setting Pitch

2.1. Setting Pitch in SoundMaker

2.1.1. Setting the Pitch of Stream Sounds and Wave Sounds

1. In SoundMaker, open the stream sound list or wave sound list.

2. Select a sound, and in the list region or parameters panel, edit the Pitch value. The specified value acts as a multiplier.

3. Use the preview feature in SoundMaker to check that the pitch has been changed.

2.1.2. Setting Instrument Pitch

1. In SoundMaker, open the Bank tab.

2. From the instrument or percussion list, select the item to edit.

3. In the list region or parameters panel, edit the Pitch (semitones) and Pitch (cents) values. The pitch will be shifted by amounts equal to the entered values.

4. Play the instrument in the SoundMaker GUI to check that the pitch has been changed.

Info

Pitch can also be changed on a per-region basis. To do this, from the Sample Map, select the region you want to change the pitch for, and at the bottom of the parameters panel, edit the values for Pitch (semitones) and Pitch (cents).

2.2 Setting Pitch in AtkPlayer

1. In the SoundParameters panel, change the value of the Pitch parameter. The specified value acts as a multiplier.

2. Play the sound from the SoundArchivePlayer panel to confirm that the pitch has been changed.
