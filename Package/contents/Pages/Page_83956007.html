About the Preview Bank Pane

The Preview Bank pane is used to determine which banks to use to play back MIDI signals that are received from a MIDI sequencer.
The banks that are registered in this pane are used for MIDI playback.

Preview Bank Operations

Opening the Preview Bank Pane

The preview bank can be opened selecting Display > Preview Bank or clicking Display Preview Bank on the toolbar.
These operations are used to switch between showing and hiding windows.

The Preview Bank pane appears as a docking window in the lower pane of the main window.

You can change the Preview Bank window to float, dock, or hide by double-clicking the Preview Bank tab at the bottom of the window or right-clicking the title bar and selecting Floating, Docking, or Hide in the shortcut menu.
You can also switch between Floating and Docking by double-clicking the title bar.

When the window is docked, you can minimize it so that only the tab is displayed by clicking the Minimize icon on the right side of the title bar, or by double-clicking the splitter.

Preview Bank Operations

Use one of the following methods to register and replace banks in the Preview Bank list.

  • Display a sequence sound list or a bank list in the Sound Set tab, select an index cell in the list, and drag the index number for that item to one of the rows in the Preview Bank pane.
  • Right-click the sequence sound list or bank list in the Sound Set tab, and select Register in Preview Bank from the shortcut menu.
    This method of registering banks always registers the selected bank in slot 0 within the Preview Bank pane.
  • You can register a bank by right-clicking an open bank tab to open the shortcut menu and selecting Register in Preview Bank.
    This method of registering banks always registers the selected bank in slot 0 within the Preview Bank pane.
  • You can register a bank from a child node with an expanded project tree bank node by right-clicking to open the shortcut menu and selecting Register in Preview Bank.
    This method of registering banks always registers the selected bank in slot 0 within the Preview Bank pane.

Registering a bank from a sequence sound list also registers the bank associated with that sequence sound.
The Preview Bank numbers (0-3) correspond to the numbers 0-3 that appear in the Multibank Settings dialog box for the sequence sound.

To delete a bank registered in the Preview Bank, select the bank to be deleted and select View > Delete, or press Delete or CTRL+D.
