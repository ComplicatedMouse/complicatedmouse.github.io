Rate of Deceleration

This page describes the Rate of Deceleration feature to decelerate or accelerate the rotation speed of emitted particles.

Overview

The Rate of deceleration feature can decelerate or accelerate rotation speed by multiplying a value specified for each frame in relation to the particle rotation speed.
For example, you can create movement like a top, which is affected by air resistance and whose rate of deceleration slows and eventually stops.

The following overview describes the settings ().

Rate of Deceleration

The rotation speed of the particle is decelerated or accelerated by multiplying the speed by a value specified for each frame.
