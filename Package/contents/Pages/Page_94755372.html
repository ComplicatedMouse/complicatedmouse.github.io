Parallel to Camera and Facing Camera

This section describes the differences between Parallel to Camera and Facing Camera.

Overview

Two different calculation methods can be used with billboard and y-axis billboard: "in the direction of the camera" and "facing the camera."
In the following image, the camera is moved in the z direction.

A Billboard_Parallel to Camera

Viewed from above the camera, as shown in the image to the left, the face of the particle is always parallel with the face of the camera lens.

When you use a primitive with thickness as the particle, more of the side becomes visible as the primitive moves toward the edge of the screen.
B Billboard_Facing Camera

Viewed from above the camera, as shown in the image to the left, the face of the particle is always oriented toward the direction of the camera.

When you use a primitive with thickness as the particle, you cannot see the side of the particle, even at the edge of the screen.
