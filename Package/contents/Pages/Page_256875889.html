When "render in two cycles" and "shadows" are used simultaneously for a special border format, there are differences in the appearance in the viewer

Question

There are differences in the appearance in the LayoutEditor and the LayoutViewer when "render in two cycles" and "shadows" are used simultaneously.

Answer

Sometimes using "render in two cycles" and "shadows" simultaneously for a special border format, in order to improve the efficiency of rendering shadows in runtime, does not yield the intended results.

Either disable one of these options or, if you want to enable both, be sure to check the results during execution (LayoutViewer).
