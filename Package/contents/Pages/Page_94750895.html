Air Resistance

This section describes the Air Resistance feature that decreases or increases the initial velocity of all emitted particles.

Overview

The Air Resistance feature multiplies the specified value by the velocity of all emitted particles per frame.
It does not affect forces applied after emission, such as fields.
The default value of 1.0 does not change the velocity. Reducing this value to lower than 1.0 decreases velocity, and increasing it to higher than 1.0 increases velocity.
The following overview describes the settings ().

Air Resistance

Speed can be decreased or increased by multiplying the velocity of the emitted particles by the set value in every frame.
