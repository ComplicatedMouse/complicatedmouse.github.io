Guide for Migrating From NW4C Graphics to NintendoSDK

Migrating Graphic Intermediate Files

Use CMDL Converter to convert NW4C graphic intermediate files (models, textures, and animations) to NintendoWare 3D intermediate files.

3dCmdlConverter.exe model.cmdl -o model.fmdb

For more information about CMDL Converter, see CMDL Converter.
