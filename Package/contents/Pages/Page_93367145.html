Emitter Shape

This section describes the Emitter Shape feature for determining the emission area for particles.

Overview

The Emitter Shape feature determines the area and direction in which particles are emitted.
When multiple particles are being emitted, changing the emitter shape can determine the overall shape of the effect.
A Point emitter shape works to display a single, unmoving particle.
The following overview describes the settings ().

Emitter Shape

Select the emitter shape from among the following presets.

Point Particles emit radially from a point.
Circle Particles emit in a disc shape from the circumference of a circle.
Circle (Evenly Divided) Particles emit in a disc shape from locations evenly divided around the circumference of a circle.
Circle (Volume) Particles emit in a disc shape from the entire face of the circle.
Sphere Particles emit in a sphere shape from around the perimeter of a sphere.
Sphere (evenly divided) Particles emit in a sphere shape from equidistant locations around a divided sphere.
Sphere (Evenly Divided 64) Particles emit in a sphere shape from locations around the perimeter of a sphere divided into anywhere from 4 (minimum) to 64 (maximum) sections.
Sphere (volume) Particles emit in a sphere shape from the entire sphere.
Cylinder Particles emit from the surface of a cylinder toward the sides of the cylinder.
Cylinder (Volume) Particles emit from the entire cylinder toward the sides of the cylinder.
Cube Particles emit from the surface of a cube toward the various faces of the cube.
Cube (Volume) Particles emit from the entire cube toward the various faces of the cube.
Lines Particles emit from the entirety of a linear emitter in the direction in which the line is drawn.
The line is drawn in the positive direction of the z-axis in world coordinates.
Line (Evenly Divided) Particles emit from locations along an evenly divided line in the direction in which the line is drawn.
Rectangle Particles emit in a rectangular shape from around the perimeter of a rectangle.
Primitive

Specifies the model file (FMDB) to load, and uses it as the emitter shape.
Particles emit in the direction of the normal of the model.
Info

A cylinder or rectangle emitter with zero thickness ignores the y-axis value set in the emitter settings when executing the effect.
