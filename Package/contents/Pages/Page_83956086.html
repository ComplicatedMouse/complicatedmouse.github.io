Preview Features

This section describes the preview features of SoundMaker.

Overview

What Is the Preview Feature?

The preview feature provides windows and panes on SoundMaker for some of the same operations to configure settings that are available in the AtkPlayer windows, for applying the configured settings in PC emulation playback, for sending the settings to AtkPlayer for playback, and for saving the settings.

After you have changed settings, perform conversion and transfer the sound archive to check the sound.

Sound Playback Operations

There are several ways to play sounds from SoundMaker, including by using the options included in the Preview menu of the main window, its toolbar buttons, and the Preview Player window, in addition to computer keyboard shortcut keys.
Note also that the playback operations differ slightly between the Sound Set tab and the Bank tab.

On the Sound Set tab, you perform operations on the sounds selected from each list.
On the Bank tab, you perform operations on the active bank tab instruments.

Sound Set Tab Operations

After selecting a sound from each list, from the Preview menu of the main window you can use the Play, Pause, and Stop options, and the toolbar buttons.
You can play back sounds by double-clicking a list, by clicking the Play button next to each item in the list, or by pressing F9. Playback can be stopped by pressing F10.

The following table lists the shortcut keys and their operations.
