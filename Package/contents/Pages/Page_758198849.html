Ribbon Bar

The Ribbon Bar contains these tabs:

Home: The primary tab, used for connecting to a dev kit, changing units, and accessing help.

Analysis: Contains ways to auto-analyze the profile.

Heartbeats: Contains the recorded or detected heartbeat code rhythms used to frame the data.

Sampled Profile: Control and settings to take a sampled profile.

Instrumented Profile: Control and settings to take an instrumented profile.

Quick Ribbon Bar: The quick controls in the top right of the Ribbon Bar.
