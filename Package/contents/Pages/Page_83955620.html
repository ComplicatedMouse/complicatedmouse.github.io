Magnetic Force

This section describes the Magnetic Force feature for attracting and repelling particles.

Overview

Magnetic Force is the field for attracting and repelling particles.
By attracting and repelling particles this way, you can make emitted particles behave like a spring.

Magnetic Force

Magnetic Force is a group () of features for configuring field settings.

Magnetic Force (±)

Sets the magnitude of the magnetic force.

Target Axes

Restricts the direction in which magnetic force is applied.
Position follows emitter Determines whether to make the position of the magnetic force follow the emitter when it moves.
Position (±) Sets the position of the magnetic force.
