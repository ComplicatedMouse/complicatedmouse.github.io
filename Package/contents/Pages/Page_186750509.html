Emitter

This page describes emitters, the source of particles.
Emitters have the following characteristics.

  • Emitters are not rendered as game effects.
  • Various parameters are set when particles are emitted, including initial velocity, lifespan, scale, and color.
  • Emitters have a variety of shapes. The position where particles are emitted and the initial velocity direction differ based on the shape.
  • There are different types of emitters based on how the particle position and speed are processed.
