Directional Billboard

This section describes the Directional Billboard particle type.

Overview

When a Directional Billboard type particle moves, it refers to its position in the previous frame in which it moved and tilts in that direction of motion.
The particle rotates only around its y-axis, so the z-plane of the particle remains parallel to the lens plane of the camera.
This can be used for effects like fireworks that burst in all directions, scaling in the direction of velocity.
