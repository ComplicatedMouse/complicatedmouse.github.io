Feel Air Resistance

This section describes the Feel Air Resistance feature for applying air resistance to GPU noise.

Overview

The Feel air resistance feature sets whether the Air Resistance value set in the Emission tab will be applied to GPU Noise.
As an example, you could use this feature and apply strong air resistance such that the field applied to particles gradually weakens.

Feel Air Resistance

Use the check box () to select whether the particles will feel the Air Resistance set in the Emission tab of the emitter node.
