Align Pane

The Align Pane is used for setting how to adjust hierarchically subordinate panes in the alignment pane.

Align Method (Horizontal)

When aligning subordinate panes, uses radio buttons to designate the locations in the Align Pane to align panes to.

Left

 Align left-justified to the left edge of the Align Pane.
Center Align centered to the center of the Align Pane.
Right Align right-justified to the right edge of the Align Pane.

Alignment Method (Vertical)

When aligning subordinate panes, uses radio buttons to designate the locations in the Align Pane to align panes to.

Up

 Align top-justified to the top edge of the Align Plane.
Center Align centered to the center of the Align Pane.
Down Align bottom-justified to the bottom edge of the Align Plane.

Default Margin

Sets the spacing between the panes being aligned.

Default Margin

 Enter the interval at which you want to place the aligned panes.

Expand End Panes

Sets whether to expand the horizontal width of subordinate panes before aligning.

Expand End Panes

When cleared (default)

Align without changing the horizontal width of subordinate panes.

Expand End Panes

When selected

Expands the horizontal width of subordinate panes, to the edges of the Align Pane before aligning.

The expanded panes are changed according to the alignment method (horizontal) settings.
Align Direction (Horizontal): Left Expands the horizontal width of panes at the left edge, to the left edge of the Align Pane.
Align Direction (Horizontal): Center

Expands the horizontal width of panes at the right and left edges, to the right and left edges of the Align Pane.
Align Direction (Horizontal): Right Expands the horizontal width of panes at the right edge, to the right edge of the Align Pane.

Align Subordinate Panes

Aligns the subordinate panes in the layout window according to the content set by pressing buttons.
