Offset Toward Camera

This section describes the Offset toward camera feature for eliminating overlapping polygons by offsetting the rendering position of the particle forward or backward relative to the camera.

Overview

The Particle Offset feature Offset toward camera enables you to manipulate the rendering position of the particle in terms of camera coordinates.
By offsetting particles in the direction of the camera, you can remove overlapping polygons, but the size of of the particles will change according to the amount of the offset and the path taken.

The following overview describes the settings ().
When you set Particle Offset to Offset toward camera, the Particle Offset settings () are displayed. The group has the following settings.

Particle Offset Value

Sets a value for the offset toward the camera, based on the position of particle emission.
To apply the offset further back behind the position of emission, specify the negative direction.
