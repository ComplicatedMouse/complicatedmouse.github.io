Alpha Offset at Beginning of Fade

This page describes the Alpha offset at beginning of fade feature, which sets the transparency of particles that overlap with the model when soft particles are processed on a vertex by vertex basis.

Overview

The Alpha offset at beginning of fade feature makes it possible to specify how large a region of a particle to set to transparent when the particle overlaps with a model.
This value is specified in terms of screen coordinates, where the coordinate values are clamped to a range of -1 to 1. As a result, the degree of transparency that is applied depends on a particle’s distance from the camera.
