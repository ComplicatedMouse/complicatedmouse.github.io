Sampling Radius

This describes the Sampling Radius feature to configure transparency by assigning a nonzero thickness from each vertex of particles when soft particles are processed on a vertex by vertex basis.

Overview

Sampling Radius is a feature that sets the radius of a circle with the vertices at the center, checks the overlap with the model inside the range taking thickness into account, and applies translucency.
Vertices that overlap with the model are softened by applying translucency.
The following overview describes the settings ().

Sampling Radius

Sets the radius of a circle with the vertices at the center using a dial () or text box () in the range of the screen coordinate system X, Y = -1 to 1.
