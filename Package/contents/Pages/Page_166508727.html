Notes

  • Preferred audio sampling rate is 48 kHz stereo. Other sample rates or mono will add additional processing to CPU
  • Concurrent decoding of up to (4) 720p streams, or (2) 1080p streams are allowed
  • Other content may be playable outside of this specification, but performance may be reduced
  • Matroska and WebM content that do not contain cue points will not be seekable
  • The movie video decoder is not expected to work correctly, if the input movie resolution is smaller than the minimum supported resolution or greater than the maximum supported resolution
