Initial Rate of History

This page describes the Initial rate of history feature that sets the initial rate of history.

Overview

The Initial rate of history feature sets an initial speed in a random direction to the history.
The following overview describes the settings ().

Initial Rate of History

Use the dial () or the text box () to specify the rate of a History.
The larger the value, the greater the movement of the History.
Also, specifying a greater value allows you to increase the amplitude when moving a History with a waveform.

Blue For more information, see the following pages.

History Change Period Sets the period of the waveform that changes in the direction of the specified axis.
Direction Change Ratio Sets the rate of change of the waveform.
