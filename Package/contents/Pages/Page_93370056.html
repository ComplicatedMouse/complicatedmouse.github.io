Emitter Shape - Cube

Describes the settings options for Emitter Shape - Cube, where particle emission locations are set about a cube.
These are the particles when this emitter shape is being used.

Overview

With the Cube emitter shape, particles emit in the direction of the normal in a widening cube shape from random positions around the outer surface of a cube located in world coordinates.
The following overview describes the settings ().

When the emitter shape is set to Cube, the Cube () settings are displayed. The group has the following settings.

Length of Side

Sets the length of the sides of the rectangle in x y z coordinates.
