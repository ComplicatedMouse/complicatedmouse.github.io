Momentum Random

This section describes the feature that randomizes the momentum of individual particles.

Overview

The Momentum Random feature randomizes the momentum of individual particles.
The speed and the effects of a variety of forces applied after the particle is emitted including gravity and fields are influenced by the momentum of each particle.
The following overview describes the settings ().

Momentum Random

Specifies the random coefficient applied to each particle at emission.
This value is multiplied by each particle's momentum every particle, and this result is added to the position.
