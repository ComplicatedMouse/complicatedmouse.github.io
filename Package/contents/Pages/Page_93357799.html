Particle Follow Type

This section describes the feature for setting how particles follow the transform animation of the emitter.

Overview

This feature sets whether emitted particles follow the position, rotation, and scale of the emitter.
The following overview describes the settings ().

Particle follow type

Select the follow type for the emitted particles from among the following presets.

Perfectly follow emitter SRT Particles after emission move in accordance with emitter transform animations.
Do not follow emitter Particles after emission do not follow emitter transform animations.
Follow only emitter position Particles after emission move in accordance with emitter position transforms only.
