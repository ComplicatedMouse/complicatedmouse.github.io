NintendoWare Audio News

This page introduces the changes in the NintendoWare audio development environment for NintendoSDK 0.14.0.

AtkPlayer

Sound playback information now can be visualized

Added a feature to visualize information about the sounds that are playing (such as the processing load of the audio renderer and the number of voices bing used) by connecting to the PC tool Spy.exe. For more information, see the section about Visualizing the Information for Playing Sounds in AtkPlayer.
