When Capture Effect (Drop Shadow) is applied, nothing is rendered in the area outside the pane size.

Question

Depending on the settings, in some cases nothing may be rendered in the area outside the pane size when Capture Effect (Drop Shadow) is applied.

Answer

Depending on the settings, when the various Capture Effect (Drop Shadow) features are enabled, only the area inside the size of the pane may be rendered, and nothing is rendered outside that size.

For example, if the Place text in a circular shape feature is used for a text box pane, text outside the pane size sometimes may not be rendered.
In such cases, adjust the Size of the base pane of the affected pane so that the text is included within the area of the size.
