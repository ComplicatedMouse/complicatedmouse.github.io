Y-Axis Billboard (Parallel to Camera)

This section describes the Y-Axis Billboard (Parallel to Camera) particle type.

Overview

For the Y-Axis Billboard (Parallel to Camera) particle type, the particle rotates only around its y-axis so that the z-plane of the particle remains parallel to the lens plane of the camera.
For example, you can use a beam effect, animating particles elongated in the direction of the y-axis, to make plane polygon particles look like cylinders.
