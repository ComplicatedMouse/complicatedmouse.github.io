Implementing Refraction Over an Appropriate Area

When you use refraction effects, you might be emitting unnecessary particles.
It is meaningless to overlap refraction effects. Adjust your parameters to use the appropriate number of particles placed appropriately over an appropriate area.


Position particles appropriately for the parts on the screen you want to refract.
If you properly position particles, you can optimize from tens of textures to just several textures, as shown in the figure.
