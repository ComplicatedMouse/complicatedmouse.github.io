XZ-plane polygon.

This section describes the XZ Plane Polygon particle type.

Overview

XY Plane Polygon type particles display with their XZ plane always matching the XZ plane in emitter coordinate space.
The particles can freely rotate because, unlike billboards, the particle's direction of rotation is not affected by the direction of the camera.
