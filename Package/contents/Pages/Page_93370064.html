Emitter Shape - Rectangle

Describes the settings for Emitter Shape - Rectangle, which specifies particle emission locations around the perimeter of a rectangle.
These are the particles when this emitter shape is being used.

Overview

With the rectangle emitter shape, particles emit in the direction of the normal in a widening rectangular shape from random positions around the circumference of the rectangle located in world coordinates.
The following overview describes the settings ().

When the emitter shape is set to Rectangle, the Rectangle () settings are displayed. The group has the following settings.

Length of Side

Sets the length of the sides of the rectangle in x z coordinates.
