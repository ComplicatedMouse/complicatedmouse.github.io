Overview

Info

This feature is available in NintendoSDK Firmware for 13.0.0-5.0 and later.

The following essential features are provided for using Bluetooth audio devices.

  • Pairing with Bluetooth audio devices
  • Displaying paired Bluetooth audio devices
  • Connecting and disconnecting Bluetooth audio devices
  • Delete the pairing information.

How to Use

+ Pairing new device

Provides the functionality to pair Bluetooth audio devices.
For more information, see Pairing.

Note
  • You cannot pair devices while the system is connected to a Bluetooth audio device.
  • Up to 10 devices can be paired.
    To pair with a new Bluetooth audio device, click Remove to delete the pairing information for one of the paired devices.

Paired Devices

Displays a list of the paired Bluetooth audio devices.

Connect

Connects to a Bluetooth audio device.
Only one Bluetooth audio device can be connected at a time.

Disconnect

Disconnects the connected Bluetooth audio device.

Remove

Deletes the pairing information.

Pairing

Provides the functionality to pair Bluetooth audio devices.

Restart Discovery

Clears the Discover Devices list and re-initiates the search for Bluetooth audio devices.

Discover Devices

Lists the Bluetooth audio devices discovered in the search.

Connect

Performs the process of pairing.
When paring succeeds, the process returns to the previous screen.
