5 Changing Parent Material and Syncing Child Materials

Change the albedo texture for the parent material (IceCream_Base) and synchronize the model’s child materials (IceCream_Chips, IceCream_Chocolate, and IceCream_Marble).

Editing the Parent Material

Double-click IceCreamMaterial to open the property window for the material.

Confirm that IceCreamMt_albIn has been added for sampler number 0.


Drag IceCreamMt_alb_A.ftxb from the following location to sampler number 0.

Location %NINTENDO_SDK_ROOT% \ Samples\Sources\Applications\G3dDemo\Resources\SampleIceCream\textures

Confirm that the color of the child materials are all synced to the parent. (Chocolate)
You can edit the parent material and change the reference targets while retaining the edits (for the toppings) you made to the child materials.

Use the same procedure to drag IceCreamMt_alb_B.ftxb and IceCreamMt_alb_C.ftxb and confirm that the color changes.
