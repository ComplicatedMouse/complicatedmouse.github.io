Spin

This section describes the Spin feature, which sets all particles moving like a twister.

Overview

Spin is a field that causes particles to rotate.
The overall effect is that of a twister.

Spin

Spin is a group () of features for configuring field settings.

Rotation Speed

This feature sets the speed of rotation.

Rotation Axis

This feature sets the emitter as the center of rotation.
Diffusion Speed This feature sets the speed at which particles disperse from the axis.
Info

A spin field causes particles to rotate around a specified axis that goes through the emitter.
The farther particles are from the rotation axis (radial distance), the farther they move (arc length).
