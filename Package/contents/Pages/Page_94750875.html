Y-Axis Initial Diffusion Velocity

This section describes the Y-Axis Initial Diffusion Velocity feature for setting the diffusion velocity from the y-axis in the emitter coordinate system.

Overview

The Y-Axis Initial Diffusion Velocity feature sets an initial velocity from the y-axis of the emitter at the time of particle emission.
Because this feature uses the emitter as a origin, the particles may be controlled by the emitter shape, or the transform settings.
The following overview describes the settings ().

Y-Axis Initial Diffusion Velocity

Sets the initial velocity of dispersion in the XZ plane of particles heading outward (or inward if the value is set to a negative number) along the y-axis of the emitter.
