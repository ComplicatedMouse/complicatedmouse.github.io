Camera Type - Perspective

This section describes the camera feature for applying perspective to give depth to an object based on the angle of view.

Overview

The Perspective feature is affected by depth information and applies perspective based on the camera's angle of view.
When Camera type is set to Perspective, the settings in the Perspective group () are displayed. The group has the following settings.
The following overview describes the settings ().

Aspect Ratio

Configures the vertical to horizontal ratio for the screen captured by the camera.

Angle of View

Configures the vertical angles of view for the image captured by the camera.
The horizontal angles of view are matched to the ratio configured in the Aspect ratio.
