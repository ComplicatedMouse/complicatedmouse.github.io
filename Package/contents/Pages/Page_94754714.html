Billboard (Facing Camera)

This section describes the Billboard (Facing Camera) particle type.

Overview

For the Billboard (Facing Camera) particle type, the z-plane of the particle faces in the direction of the camera and the particle rotates with its y-axis constrained by the direction of the y-axis of the camera.
This can be used to reduce the warping of particles at the edges of the screen due to perspective.
