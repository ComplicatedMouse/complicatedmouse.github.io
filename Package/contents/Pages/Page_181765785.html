Directional Billboard Particles Are Not Oriented Toward Camera

When you use Directional Billboard type particles, there are situations where the particle's polygon faces are not oriented toward the camera.
This section describes the situations and ways to deal with the issue.

The following effect uses Directional Billboard type particles.
The particles emitting from the regions marked with 〇 face toward the camera and display correctly.
The particles emitting from the sites marked with × do not face toward the camera. The particles emitting from the sites at 3 o'clock and 9 o'clock cannot be seen at all.

The Problem As shown in the following image, which depicts the view looking down at the camera, the particle's polygon faces do not have a defined orientation when the direction in which the particles are moving (the rotation axis) is the same as the camera axis.

　

Solution 1 Set Omnidirectional Initial Velocity (±) to 0.01 so all particles faces are oriented toward the camera.
