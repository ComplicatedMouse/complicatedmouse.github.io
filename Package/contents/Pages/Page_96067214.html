Taking Advantage of Glare and Bloom

Use glare and bloom to depict luminescence. Avoid the strategy of expressing luminescence by overwriting using the Additive translucent blend type.
To increase luminescence by duplicating emitters, as shown below, you must fill roughly twice the area.

If you can use glare or bloom effects, you can depict luminescence while economizing on the fill area by entering values of 1.0 or greater for the color values and color scale values.
