Dealing With Installed NX Fonts

Scope of License

This font can be used to develop and create software and content for the NX and its associated documentation (including products for sales and distribution via electronic media). Do not use it for any other purpose.

Although you may modify this font as necessary, when you make modifications after first converting to a bitmap font, you accept all responsibility for any problems that your modifications cause.

Contact Nintendo support if you have any other licensing questions.

Cautions for Management

This font may not be sold, loaned, transferred, or distributed to a third party, nor may a third party use it in any way, without the approval of the font's manager at Nintendo.

If requested by Nintendo, you must delete or return this font immediately.

Usage in NintendoWare

The BFTTF, XLOR, and XLLT files are included in the Resources/Fonts/NX directory of the NintendoWare NX Add-On package.
