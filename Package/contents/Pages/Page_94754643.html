Particle Center

This section describes the feature for setting the center position for particle scaling and rotation.

Overview

The Particle Center feature allows you to set a position in the XY plane based on the center of the particle that will serve as the center for particle scaling and rotation.
Setting a value greater than 1 moves the center position outwards beyond the plane of the screen, which makes it possible to achieve large rotations.
The following overview describes the settings ().

Particle Center

Use the dial () or the text box () to specify the particle center (where 1 is the entire particle).
You can specify a "particle center" outside of the actual particle by specifying a numerical value outside of the range from -1.0 through 1.0.
