Particle Shape - Particle

This section describes the Particle setting for particle shape.

Overview

When Particle is specified as the particle shape, rectangular polygons are generated automatically in the effect runtime.
The generated polygons hold the following attribute information.

Vertex positions The coordinates for the positions of the four vertices of a rectangle of size 1x1.
Normals The normal is in the direction from the surface of the rectangle toward the viewer.
Tangent and binormal The tangent is toward the right, and the binormal is in the upward direction.
Texture Coordinates The coordinates of the texture that is planar mapped to the surface of the rectangle.
Vertex color The vertex color is fixed to RBGA = 1.0.
