Gravity - Apply in World Coordinates

Apply in World Coordinates

This section describes the Apply in world coordinates feature for applying gravity using world coordinates.

Overview

When emitters are rotated, Apply in world coordinates is a feature to always apply acceleration in a specified direction without influence by the particles.
All emitted particles can fall downwards, even during emitter rotation animations.

ON

Particles always have acceleration applied in the negative y-axis direction (straight down) using world coordinates.
OFF Particles always have acceleration applied in the negative y-axis direction relative to the emitter itself.
Particles are also affected by the emitter’s rotation animation.
