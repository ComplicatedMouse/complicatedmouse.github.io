Coefficient Animation

This section describes the Coefficient animation feature that animates the amount of refraction in Color Buffer Refraction and the amount of distortion in Texture Distortion.

Overview

The Coefficient animation feature animates the amount of refraction in Color Buffer Refraction and the amount of distortion in Texture Distortion.
The following overview describes the settings ().

Coefficient Animation

Animate the value multiplied by the coefficient in the Gradation Editor ().
