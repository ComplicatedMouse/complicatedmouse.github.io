Wrap Settings to Use During Sampling

The wrap settings to use in the u, v, and w directions during texture sampling are specified by drop-down lists on the Samplers page of the material Property Window.

Wrap Type Description
Repeat Repeats the texture.
Mirror Flips and repeats the texture.
Clamp Stretches pixels at the edges of the texture.
Mirror once Repeats only once in the negative texture coordinate direction and then stretches pixels at the edges of the texture.
