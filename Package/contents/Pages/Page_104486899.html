Connecting Controllers in a PC Environment (Adding Devices)

Overview

To use the controller on your PC, you must use Windows functionality to add the controller as a device on your PC. (Bluetooth pairing will occur.)

If a controller has been added to a PC and then paired in another environment (PC, SDEV), you must perform Delete Device to reconnect it to the original PC.

Procedure

  1. Open Control Panel. In Devices and Printers, click Add a device.



  2. Press and hold the SYNC Button on the controller for one second or longer. The LED flashes when pairing begins on the controller.
    NintendoGamepadXX appears in the Add a device window. Select the icons shown, and click Next.
    The "XX" part differs depending on the controller.



  3. The following screen appears after the devices have been successfully added. You can then reconnect the PC and the controller, if they lose connection, by pressing any button other than the SYNC Button.

    Warning

    The LED continues to flash even after the controller and PC are connected, until an application you built with the NX Add-On is run.
    The first time the application starts, a controller number is fixed so that the LED stops flashing and the LED associated with the controller number illuminates.

Deleting Controller Registration

When you want to use a controller that has been connected to one PC with a different PC, we recommend removing the controller as a device from the PC you are no longer using.

  1. From Control Panel, click Devices and Printers.


  2. From the device list, select the controllers you want to remove, and then click Remove device.


  3. Select Yes to remove the device information from the PC.
