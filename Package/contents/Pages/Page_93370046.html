Emitter Shape - Cylinder

Describes the settings for Emitter Shape - Cylinder, where particle emission locations are set around the circumference of a cylinder.
These are the particles when this emitter shape is being used.

Overview

With the Cylinder emitter shape, particles emit in the direction of side wall from random positions around the circumference of the cylinder located in world coordinates.
The following overview describes the settings ().

When the emitter shape is set to Cylinder, the Cylinder settings are displayed. The group has the following settings.

Emitter Radius

Sets the radius of the cylinder.

Arc Width (Angle)

Sets the rotating arc by the angle set in Arc starting point (angle) on the axis through the center of the circle at the top or bottom of the cylinder.

Arc Starting Angle

Sets the arc starting point of the top or bottom of the cylinder with an angle. For the default value, the z-coordinate in the positive direction serves as the starting place.

Random Arc Starting Angle

You can enable or disable the randomization of the arc starting angle of the top or bottom of the cylinder by selecting or clearing the check box ().
