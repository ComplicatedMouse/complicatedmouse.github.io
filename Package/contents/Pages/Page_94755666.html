Billboard and Directional

This section describes the differences between Billboard and Directional.

Overview

With a Directional type particle, the speed of the particle affects the direction of the particle.
The following images compare Billboard and Directional particles. Speed is applied in all directions to the emitters together.

A Billboard This looks strange for particles with directionality.
B Directional Billboard Particles tend toward the direction of velocity, so this is useful for particles with directionality.
Info

Directional particles that depend on particle speed do not display when the particle speed is zero.
