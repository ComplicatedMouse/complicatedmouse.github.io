This page describes the changes in NintendoSDK 5.3.0.

SoundMaker, Command-Line Tools

Waveform conversion in SoundMaker and SoundProjectWaveConverter has been improved to convert waveforms until the end of playback

Waveform conversion in SoundMaker and SoundProjectWaveConverter has been improved to convert waveforms until the end of playback.

Previously, conversion to a waveform terminated during playback, but now conversion is performed until playback is complete.

As a result of this fix, note that the output results and the end portion of waveform conversion in previous versions of SoundMaker and SoundProjectWaveConverter may be different.
