Particle Type

This section describes the Particle Type feature for setting the orientation of particles.

Overview

The Particle Type feature sets the orientation of particles.
A number of presets have been prepared, including one to orient particles in the direction of the camera, and another to orient particles depending on their velocity.
Select the preset that fits the effect you want to create.
The following overview describes the settings ().

Particle Type

Select the particle type from among the following presets.

Billboard (Parallel to Camera)

The z-plane of the particle remains parallel to the lens plane of the camera, or the particle rotates with its y-axis constrained by the y-axis of the camera.
Billboard (Facing Camera)

The z-plane of the particle faces in the direction of the camera and the particle rotates with its y-axis constrained by the direction of the y-axis of the camera.
Y-Axis Billboard (Parallel to Camera)

The particle rotates only around its y-axis so the z-plane of the particle remains parallel to the lens plane of the camera.
Y-Axis Billboard (Facing Camera)

The particle rotates only around its y-axis, with the z-plane of the particle facing the camera.
XY-Plane Polygon The particle is shown so that its xy-plane matches the xy-plane in emitter coordinate space.
XZ-Plane Polygon

The particle is shown so that its xz-plane matches the xy-plane in emitter coordinate space.
Directional Billboard

The moving particle tilts relative to its direction of movement in the previous frame.
The particle rotates only around its y-axis so the z-plane of the particle remains parallel to the lens plane of the camera.
Directional Polygon The moving particle tilts relative to its direction of movement in the previous frame.
