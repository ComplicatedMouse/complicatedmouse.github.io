Texture Details Window

This window is used to confirm color or alpha details about the selected texture image.
To open the Texture Information window, click the texture thumbnail registered in the Texture tab.

A Magnification of the displayed texture image.
B Enlarges or reduces the texture image size.
C Channel of the displayed texture image.
D Shows a mipmap display of the texture image.
E Switches the display method for mipmap.
F Displays Help.
G Shows the texture image.
H The texture format.
I The mipmap level.
J File size of the texture image. Unit of measurement for the texture image (bytes).
