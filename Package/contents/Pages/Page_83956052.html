What Is Preview Player?

The Preview Player pane is used in SoundMaker to check sounds by sending the settings to the console. In operation, it corresponds to the SoundArchivePlayer panel in AtkPlayer.

When the Preview Player plays the data, the sounds are played by PC emulation, or by AtkPlayer if connected to AtkPlayer.

Preview Player Operations

Opening Preview Player

You can open the Preview Player by selecting View > Preview Player or by clicking Show Preview Player in the toolbar.

The shortcut ALT4 can also be used for this operation. These operations are used to switch between showing and hiding windows.

The Preview Player pane appears as a docking window in the lower pane of the main window.

You can change the Preview Player window to float, dock, or hide by selecting Floating, Docking, or Hide in the Preview Player tab at the bottom of the window, or from the title bar's shortcut menu.
You can also switch between Floating and Docking by double-clicking the title bar.

When the window is docked, you can minimize it so that only the tab is displayed by clicking the Minimize icon on the right side of the title bar, or by double-clicking the splitter.

Preview Player Operations

Use one of the following options to register and replace sounds in the play list for Preview Player.

  • Click a selected index cell in the Sound Set tab, and drag it to the play list for Preview Player.
  • Select a sound in a list within the Sound Set tab, and then play it by clicking Play in the list or the toolbar, or by pressing F9 or the space bar. The selected sound is then registered in the selected slot within the play list in the Preview Player window, and can be played from there.

　　　　　

Separately hold down the Play button, the Pause button and the Stop button to check the sounds that have been registered to the play list.
As shortcut keys, you can use the SPACEBAR and F9 for Play and F10 for Stop All.

To play the sound back as a loop, hold down the Loop playback button.
To turn that setting on and off, click the button again.
