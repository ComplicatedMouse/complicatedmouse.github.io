Emitter Shape - Sphere (Volume)

Describes the settings for Emitter Shape - Sphere (volume), where particle emission locations are set for a sphere.
These are the particles when this emitter shape is being used.

Overview

With the Sphere (Volume) emitter shape, particles emit in the direction of the normal in a widening sphere shape from the entire surface of the sphere.
The following overview describes the settings ().

When the emitter shape is set to Sphere (volume), the Sphere (volume) () settings are displayed. The group has the following settings.

Hollowness

Specifies a region from which particles are not emitted as a percentage from the center of the emitter.
