Rounded Rectangle Shape Picture Pane

Shape Settings

Configures the rounded rectangle shape of the picture pane.

When the picture pane has a rounded rectangle shape, the four corners of the pane are configured to have a rounded shape.
These settings configure the size and number of angular divisions of the rounded corners.

Rounded corner radius (px): Enter the size of the four rounded corners of the picture pane.
Angular divisions:

Enter the number of angular divisions of the four rounded corners of the picture pane.

More divisions makes for smoother rounding, but also increases the processing load.

Note: The size of the rounded corners of a rounded rectangle cannot be animated.
