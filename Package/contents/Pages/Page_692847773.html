How do the LayoutEditor operations involved in re-exporting differ from each other?

Question

What are the differences between the various LayoutEditor re-exporting operations?

Answer

Any changes made to a parts layout by any of the following operations will be applied to the body layout and to derivative parts. The targets and the changes that are made are different.
