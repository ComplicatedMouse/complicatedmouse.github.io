Fixing the Box in Front of the Camera

This section describes the feature for fixing the box in front of the camera.

Overview

This feature fixes the position of the box relative to direction of the camera viewpoint.
The distance between the box and the camera remains the same, even when the camera is moved forward and backward.
This section provides an overview of the settings ().

Fixing the Box in Front of the Camera

The position, scale, and rotation of the box are applied using camera coordinates.
By having the box follow the camera position, you can achieve the following kinds of effects.

  • Have rain fall only in a specific area.
  • Have rain falling throughout the level, but limit the rendering of the effect to the area around the camera to reduce the load on the system.

Internal Implementation

Using this feature changes the coordinate system used for calculations.
The following figure illustrates the difference in coordinate system when the feature is enabled and disabled.

A Fixing the Box in Front of the Camera - disabled World coordinates > "box" coordinates
B Fixing the Box in Front of the Camera - enabled World coordinates > camera coordinates > box coordinates
