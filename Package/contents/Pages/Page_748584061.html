Find Hotspots within a Function

Once you have identified a function to speed up, the next step is to analyze the internals of the function.

This workflow explains how to find look at profile data for individual assembly instructions.


  1. On the Functions tab, right click on a function you want to inspect and choose Show Assembly. Alternatively, you can make sure the Assembly tab is visible and left click on any function in the Functions tab.
  2. Go to the Assembly tab and click the Show Source button in the toolbar. This loads your source code and aligns it with the assembly code.
  3. Scroll downward to find hotspots. In the example below, the lines of assembly with the most samples in the Self column have the largest pink bar.
