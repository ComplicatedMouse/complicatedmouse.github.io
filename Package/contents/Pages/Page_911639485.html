Blending processing occurs twice for the capture effect and capture pane.

Question

The alpha part changes in the text box pane with the Brilliance capture effect added, and the capture texture that results from that capture effect being added to the capture pane.
The following images compares the results of applying the effects.

Rendering of Capture Effect Only Rendering of Capture Texture Generated With Capture Effect Included in Capture Pane

Answer

The capture texture result varies because blending is applied twice: once by the blending process of the capture effect Rendering Mode, and once by the blending process of the capture pane.

Capture effects are alpha-blended and rendered when the pane and those effects are rendered.
When this pane is included in the capture pane, it is further alpha-blended when the capture texture is rendered.
