Near Alpha

This section describes the Near Alpha feature that applies translucency to particles when they are in near distance of the camera.

Overview

The Near Alpha feature applies an alpha value of 0.0 to particles within the vertex shader when they are in near distance of the camera.
This can resolve display problems and rendering load problems from penetration occurring by particles with a large rendered surface area stretching over the camera.

Distance where Alpha is '0'

Sets the distance from the camera where the alpha value becomes 1.
