How can I adjust overly large margins to the left and right of the glyph for Cyrillic and other text?

Question

Cyrillic and other text sometimes have overly large margins to the left and right of the glyph. Is there a way to adjust the margins?

Answer

You can create a more natural appearance by setting deleteBearingX in the FCPX file to true to adjust the bearing (margin to the left and right) of the glyph. You can also increase and decrease bearingOffsetX in the FCPX file to adjust the bearing.

For more information, see Composite Font Definition File Format.
