Mask Textures

Mask textures are used for facial expressions.

Textures for eyebrows, eyes, mustache, mouth, and moles are all rendered for the mask texture. A mask texture is rendered separately for each facial expression type.

Parts of a Mask Texture

A mask texture is composed of a number of parts, combined together to render the mask texture according to the parameters configured in the Mii Data (the CharInfo data structure). The color and alpha components are rendered separately so that the outlines are sharply drawn in the generated image.

The nn::mii::Mask class serves to manage the shape and texture data for rendering the mask texture of an individual Mii character.

The following table shows the relationships between the various mask parts and the corresponding nn::mii::Mask::DrawType enumerated types.

Type nn::mii::Mask::DrawType Comments
Mustache

nn::mii::Mask::DrawType_MustacheR

nn::mii::Mask::DrawType_MustacheL

The left and right parts are rendered separately.
Mouth nn::mii::Mask::DrawType_Mouth
Eyebrows

nn::mii::Mask::DrawType_EyebrowR

nn::mii::Mask::DrawType_EyebrowL

The left and right parts are rendered separately.
Not all Mii characters have eyebrows parts.
Eyes

nn::mii::Mask::DrawType_EyeR

nn::mii::Mask::DrawType_EyeL

The left and right parts are rendered separately.
Mole nn::mii::Mask::DrawType_Mole Not all Mii characters have a mole part.

The eyebrow and moles parts do not need to be rendered for some Mii characters. For information about implementation-related precautions regarding unnecessary parts, see the description of Mask.
